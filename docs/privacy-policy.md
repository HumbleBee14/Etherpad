# Privacy Policy for Etherpad

**Last updated:** May 23, 2026

## Overview

Etherpad ("the App") is developed and published by Dinesh Yadav ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information collected through the App.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Etherpad is a standalone music synthesis application that runs entirely on your device. The App does not:

- Collect personal information
- Track your usage or behavior
- Use analytics or telemetry services
- Connect to any external servers
- Store data on remote servers
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- Access your contacts, photos, location, or other personal data
- Include advertising or third-party tracking SDKs
- Require an account or sign-in

## Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you configure within the App are stored **locally on your device only**. This data never leaves your device.

## Audio and Microphone

Etherpad **does not access your microphone**. The App generates sound through internal synthesis only.

## Third-Party Services

Etherpad does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
